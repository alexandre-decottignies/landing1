Who is Smoke ?

Welcome to Smoke: Where Culinary Excellence Meets Luxury

Indulge your senses and embark on a gastronomic journey like no other at Smoke, the epitome of opulence nestled in the heart of London. As a beacon of exquisite taste and unparalleled luxury, Smoke transcends the ordinary, inviting you to experience the extraordinary. Step into a world where culinary artistry, refined ambiance, and exceptional service converge, creating a symphony of sensations that will leave an everlasting imprint on your soul.

A Culinary Odyssey

At Smoke, we believe that dining is not just a necessity; it is an experience to be savored, cherished, and remembered. Our culinary artisans, under the guidance of renowned chefs, meticulously curate a menu that tantalizes the taste buds and ignites the imagination. Each dish is a masterpiece, a fusion of premium, locally-sourced ingredients and innovative techniques that celebrate the essence of flavors. From succulent steaks to delicately crafted seafood, every bite tells a story of passion, expertise, and a relentless pursuit of perfection.

Exquisite Ambiance

The moment you step through our doors, you are enveloped in an atmosphere of elegance and sophistication. The interior, adorned with subtle hues, plush furnishings, and ambient lighting, sets the stage for an unforgettable dining experience. Whether you're here for an intimate dinner, a celebratory soirée, or a corporate gathering, our space effortlessly adapts to the occasion, ensuring that every moment is imbued with grandeur and charm.

Steak Menu

Avocado menu

Garden's Bounty on Your Plate: Dive into Our Avocado Delight, Adorned with an Array of Toppings - Think Roasted Pine Nuts, Bursting Pomegranate Seeds, and a Splash of Balsamic Glaze. This Dish, Born from Roman Tradition, Captures the Essence of Italy on Your Plate, Offering a Heavenly Union of Creaminess and Umami Goodness.
